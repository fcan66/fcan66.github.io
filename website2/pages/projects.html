    Welcome to Projects in Progress Title VII Indian Education Program


    Please read about our projects in progress. We need the support of the community to ensure success.





    The Community Technology Center (CTC) is a facility that provides free computer access and training to people without a computer or internet in their home. In the Native American worldview, a CTC is a place where parents and guardians as well as their children find a space to meet their educational technology needs, be informed of therapeutic services, receive cultural services, obtain foster parent certification, and acquire advocacy. In addition, the CTC plans to host community meetings and welcome visitors interested in connecting with the Native American community. The main objective of CTC is to provide support and tools for the family and community.
